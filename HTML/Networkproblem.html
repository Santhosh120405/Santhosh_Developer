Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time :15 minutes

Cook Time:10 minutes

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 oF (180 oc)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocolate and chips and stir well
  4. bake for 10 minutes or util golden brown
Ingredients
ingredient Quantity
flour 2cup
Sugar 1cup
Chocolate pieces 1cup
